Where you will find blocks: City of Miami map.

Where you will find block groups: Miami-Dade County map.

These data were obtained from the US Census Bureau. The shapefile for Miami-Dade county was downloaded from the Census's TIGER/Line Shapefiles. Demographic information from the 2010 Decennial Census was downloaded at the block level from the Census API. At the block group level, 2018 population estimates, household income, and homeownership data were obtained from the ACS.
