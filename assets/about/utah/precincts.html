These data were obtained from the Utah Automated Geographic Reference Center (AGRC), the MIT Elections Data and Science Lab (MEDSL), and National Historic GIS (NHGIS). The precinct shapefile was downloaded from the Utah AGRC and 2016 election results were downloaded from the MEDSL. Demographic information from the 2010 Decennial Census was downloaded at the block level from NHGIS. The source shapefile for this module, along with additional information about processing, is available for download from MGGG's GitHub organization @mggg-states.
