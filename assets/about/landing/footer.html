About Districtr

Districtr is a free community webtool for redistricting and community mapping provided by the MGGG Redistricting Lab at Tufts University. We welcome questions and inquiries about the tool and our work. We are also happy to work with classes and other educational and research groups to create custom modules. Reach out to us at: contact@mggg.org
