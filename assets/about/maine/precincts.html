The Maine precinct shapefile was obtained from the Maine Geolibrary and processed by members of the MGGG Redistricting Lab (MGGG). Election data come from the Maine Bureau of Corporations, Elections, & Commissions. Data from absentee votes reported at the state level were disaggregated by voting age population. Because of data reporting and processing, a few precincts cross county boundaries. Demographic data for the 2010 Decennial Census were retrieved using the Census API.
