The units you see here are precincts.

Raw data for Connecticut's voting district boundaries was sourced from election and other town officials and processed by members of the MGGG Redistricting Lab. Precinct-level election results were obtained from the Connecticut Secretary of State's Election Center.

Block level demographic data for the 2010 Decennial Census were retrieved using the Census API. Demographic data were aggregated from the census block level and voting districts were assigned to districts using MGGG's proration software, MAUP.
