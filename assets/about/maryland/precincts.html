The units you see here are precincts. This shapefile was obtained from the Maryland Department of Planning website and processed by members of the Metric Geometry and Gerrymandering Group (MGGG). The Maryland precinct shapefile was obtained from the Maryland Department of Planning's Redistricting website. Election data come from the Maryland State Board of Elections. Statewide election returns from the Board of Elections were cleaned by MGGG staff in order to join them to the precinct shapefile.
