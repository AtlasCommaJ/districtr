In March 2019 the US Supreme Court heard oral arguments for Rucho v. Common Cause, in which plaintiffs claim that North Carolina's remedial 2016 congressional map is an unconstitutional partisan gerrymander. The 2016 remedial map had been drawn after the Supreme Court struck down two of the state's 2011 enacted congressional districts as unconstitutional racial gerrymanders. MGGG and our collaborators filed an amicus brief which can be accessed on our website.
