These data were obtained from the North Carolina General Assembly and National Historic GIS (NHGIS). The VTD shapefile and election results were downloaded from the North Carolina General Assembly's 2016 Redistricting Reference Data. Demographic information from the 2010 Decennial Census was downloaded at the block level from NHGIS. The source shapefile for this module, along with additional information about processing, is available for download from MGGG's GitHub organization @mggg-states.
