Where you will find precincts: Statewide maps

The precinct shapefile is made available on Kaggle by Garret Archer, formerly Senior Elections Analyst with the Arizona Secratary of State and currently a Data Analyst with ABC15 Arizona. Tabular election data are from the MIT Election Data Science Lab. Block level demographic data for the 2010 Decennial Census were retrieved using the Census API. Processing was completed by members of the Metric Geometry and Gerrymandering Group (MGGG).

Some very limited merging of precincts in the original shapefile was necessary to join election results. La Paz County precincts were edited to match data provided on the county's election website.

Native American Tribes and Nations come from the US Census's American Indian Areas shapefile.
