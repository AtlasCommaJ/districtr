Virginia Beach

Welcome to Districtr! This is a free community webtool for redistricting and community mapping provided by the MGGG Redistricting Lab at Tufts University. We welcome questions and inquiries about the tool and about our work in Virginia Beach: contact@mggg.org

Try Drawing Districts

We invite you to use Districtr to draw your own districts and think about some of the tradeoffs for yourself. You can build 3 City Council Districts, or 7 City Council Districts using census blocks or precincts as the basic building blocks.
